Our story so far

Non-traditional is the theme here just like Chris and I’s relationship. We met at our previous place of work and bonded over fitness and beer. We were competing in a weight loss competition and instead of a fierce duel, it turned into us motivating and encouraging each other. We ended up getting first and second place but gained something much better. Not only a partner in the gym but, also in life. Over the summer and throughout our relationship we have overcame a lot of hurdles, but each one brought us closer together. Annalisa and Chris have bond and I am so very happy she gets to have another wonderful male role model who absolutely adores her. During our relationship we have changed jobs, moved homes, travelled to different countries (during a hurricane haha), and have gotten a new family member- our wonderful kitten Curry. Life is not easy, but it feels great knowing we are holding hands and running towards our goals together at full speed.

A wedding at a brewery?

Jester King Brewery is one of our favorite places, why? There are a number of reasons, but let's start at the beginning. Take a little trip past downtown Austin and enjoy the winding, hilly roads as you make your way towards this tucked away farm and brewery. As you approach, you will soon see that tradition has been thrown out the window.

There's the taproom on the hill, and below that, a farm-to-table pizzeria connected to another bar. You will hear the buzz of excitement of the adults, children, and dogs. Then you will be hit by the intoxicating aroma of the artisan pizzas and funky farmhouse ales. As you journey upwards, you can take some time to tour the brewery, with huge vats of delicious beer; or just snag some sweet merch instead. Then, go down the hill, pick up your pizza and watch the children play in the sand, or the pups bounding after Frisbees in the vast fields.

Show up on a Saturday and you'll get a special treat - meeting Farmer Peppy, his goats, and Sasha the sheepdog (goatdog?). Farmer Peppy will explain his unique agricultural vision for Jester King. This includes the vineyards, the apiary, the orchard, and his do-nothing style of farming - it's truly a fun and educational behind-the-scenes tour. The best part? It ends with petting and picking up goats! Stop by and you'll often find our family joyfully doing any number of these activities.

We fell in love with Jester King almost instantly, it was the first of many breweries we visited as a couple and we are so excited it will be the first place we celebrate being husband and wife. Annalisa is a fan too! She loves to play in the sandbox, drink lemonade, eat cookies, and befriend every dog in sight. Our family is the most happy here and we can't wait to share this happiness with ya’ll as you join us on our special day.
